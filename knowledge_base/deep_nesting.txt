Deep Nesting

Definition
Deep nesting occurs when multiple if, for, while, or switch statements are nested.

Problems
- Difficult to understand
- High cyclomatic complexity
- Hard to debug
- Difficult to maintain

Example

Bad:
if():
    if():
        if():
            if():

Better:
Use helper methods.
Use early returns.
Split logic into smaller functions.

Severity
Medium
